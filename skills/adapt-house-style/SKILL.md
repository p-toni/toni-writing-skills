# HOUSE STYLE ADAPTER — Surface Transformation Only

## Purpose
Apply consistent house style without touching substance.

## Instructions

Rewrite the document to match house style.

Rules:
- Headings must be sentence case (EN/PT)
- No em dashes. Use colons or parentheses instead
- Remove filler transitions (“overall”, “in summary”, “it’s important to note”)
- Cut 15–25% of length
- Keep these lines verbatim and near the top (if present):
  - My take:
  - The core tension is:
  - Constraint that matters:
  - We stop if:
  - What would change my mind:

## Hard Constraints

- Do not change claims
- Do not change decisions
- Do not change metrics
- Do not restructure logic
- Output the edited memo only
